## Description (4000 chars max)

Haven is a fast, private note-taking app built for people who think clearly.

Write in Markdown with live formatting — bold, italic, headers, code blocks, and [[wiki links]] all render as you type. No buttons to click. Just write.

WRITE BEAUTIFULLY
Haven's editor renders Markdown inline. Type **bold** and see it bold. Type # Header and see it large. Type [[Note Title]] to link your ideas together. It's how writing should feel — immediate, distraction-free, and beautiful.

SEE YOUR IDEAS CONNECT
Every [[wiki link]] creates a connection. Open the Knowledge Graph to see your notes as a visual network — tap any node to jump into that note. Discover relationships between ideas you didn't know existed.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Your notes live on your device. Haven has zero analytics, zero tracking, and zero AI reading your thoughts. Optional sync uses AES-256 end-to-end encryption — not even Haven's servers can read your notes.

ORGANIZE YOUR WAY
Folders, tags, and wiki links — three ways to organize without being forced into one system. Pin important notes to the top. Search instantly with full-text search.

CAPTURE EVERYTHING
• Speak your thoughts — built-in voice-to-text transcription
• Daily Note — auto-created template for tasks, notes, and reflections
• Import from Notion — bring your existing notes in one tap
• Quick Capture widget — jot a note from your home screen

BUILT FOR FOCUS
Haven is intentionally simple. No databases. No AI assistants. No feature bloat. One subscription, one price, no upsells. Built by one person who was tired of bloated note apps.

HAVEN PRO
Free: Unlimited local notes, folders, tags, wiki links, knowledge graph, markdown editor, voice transcription, daily notes.

Pro ($1.99/month or $19.99/year): Cloud sync across devices with end-to-end encryption.

Your notes. Nobody else's.

## App Review Notes

Haven is a note-taking app. The subscription (Haven Pro) unlocks cloud sync with end-to-end encryption. All other features are free.

To test subscriptions, use the StoreKit Testing environment in Xcode.

The sync server is optional — the app works fully offline without it. Users who enable sync connect to their own server (self-hosted).

No login is required to use the app. Face ID lock is optional and can be enabled in Settings → Security.
