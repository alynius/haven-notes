Frequently asked questions

Can I use Haven for free?
Yes. Haven is free to download and use with no time limit. All core features — local notes, Markdown editing, wiki links, knowledge graph, tags, folders, daily notes, voice dictation, full-text search, and widgets — are included at no cost. Pro is only needed if you want to sync across devices.
What does Pro add?
Haven Pro unlocks cloud sync and end-to-end encrypted sync. Your notes are encrypted on your device before leaving it, so even your sync server can't read them. Pro is a subscription — monthly or yearly — billed through your Apple ID.
How do I cancel?
You can cancel anytime from your iPhone: go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, and select Haven. Cancelling stops future charges; you keep Pro access until the current period ends. Your notes are never deleted — they remain on your device.
Is my data private?
All notes are stored locally on your device. Haven does not operate servers that hold your data. If you enable sync, you provide your own server URL — Haven never sees your notes. With E2E encryption enabled, your notes are encrypted on-device using AES-GCM before syncing, so even server access reveals nothing.
