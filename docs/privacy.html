Haven Notes

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

The Short Version

Haven is built on a simple principle: your notes are yours. We don't collect, store, or transmit your data. Period.

Data Storage

All notes, tags, folders, and tasks are stored locally on your device in an SQLite database. Haven does not operate any servers that receive your data.

Optional Sync

If you enable sync, your notes are sent to a server URL that you provide and control. Haven never connects to any Haven-operated server. If you enable encryption, notes are encrypted with AES-256-GCM on your device before being transmitted. Not even your sync server can read encrypted notes.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not collect usage analytics or telemetry
  • We do not collect crash reports
  • We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We have no user accounts or registration

Microphone & Speech Recognition

Haven includes an optional voice dictation feature. When enabled, audio is processed using Apple's on-device Speech Recognition framework. Audio data is not sent to Haven and is not stored. On-device processing is preferred when available.

Face ID / Touch ID

Haven offers optional biometric lock. Authentication is handled entirely by Apple's LocalAuthentication framework on your device. No biometric data is accessed or stored by Haven.

In-App Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. Haven does not receive or store payment information.

Children's Privacy

Haven does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains no features that target children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version here with an updated date. Material changes will be communicated through the app's update notes.
